About the Department of Physics

Rangia College

Our History

The Department of Physics at Rangia College was established in 1985 with the vision of providing quality education in the field of physics. Over the years, the department has grown and evolved, becoming a cornerstone of scientific inquiry and excellence in the region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a deep understanding of physical principles and encourage critical thinking, innovation, and research among students. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in various scientific fields and contribute positively to society.

Key Highlights

Experienced Faculty: Our faculty members are highly qualified and dedicated to student success, providing mentorship and guidance.

State-of-the-Art Laboratory: The department boasts a modern laboratory equipped with advanced instruments for practical learning.

Research Opportunities: Students are encouraged to engage in research projects, collaborating with faculty on cutting-edge studies.

Community Outreach: We actively participate in community education programs, promoting science awareness and literacy.
