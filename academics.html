National Education Policy (NEP)

The National Education Policy (NEP) aims to transform the education system in India by promoting a holistic and multidisciplinary approach to learning. Under NEP, the FYUGP (Four-Year Undergraduate Program) is introduced, allowing students greater flexibility and choice in their subjects. This initiative enhances the learning experience and prepares students for a rapidly changing world.
